Making Cupcakes from store Cake Mix and Frosting

  1. Preheat the Oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine, eggs, oil, water, and fry mix together
    • TIP: Add and extra egg to the mix to make the cupcakes less dense and more moist.
    • TIP: If you want to add extra ingredients
      • like lemon juice to make lemon cupcakes, substitute some of the water amount with lemon juice.
  3. Place cupcakes liners line cupcake tins and fill 3/4 of the liners
  4. Put cupcakes in the oven for 10-15 minutes are until a toothpick come out clean
  5. Stick store frosting in the fridge to thicken
  6. Pull cupcakes out of cupcake pan and onto a cooling rack
  7. Once cupcakes are cool pull frosting out of the fridge and stick it into a pipping bacg or ziplock bag.
  8. Pipe whatever designs and styles you want to try
